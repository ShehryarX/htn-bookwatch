About

Hello! We are BookWatch and we also love bibliophilism. It's great to see you here; get started taking a picture of a book you've discovered, and choose what's best for you. Through our application, you can easily organize your books and more importantly, enjoy the books you love. In addition, we will even give you suggestions based on the contents you prefer through our new artifical intelligence program.

Features

OCR Recognition

Using Optical Character Recognition, our application gives you the ability to easily search information of the book you may be interested in, at a glance. This feature is powered through Tesseract.

Ease of Access Decisions

We help you choose your books by learning the patterns you experience whilst reading various works of reading. Our goal is ensure that we suggest what you love.
